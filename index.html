Some of our latest work

Correction + 2Yr Ceramic

Porsche Cayman S

Craig has been a loyal of ours over the years. When we booked in his Cayman S, after an initial inspection we knew the paint had seen a little more abuse than anticipated.

A steady 2 stage correction (heavy cut -> light cut) lifted the swirls out of the paintwork, followed by a few localised corrections like bird poo etching and heavier scratches. This involved 1500g - 3000g wet sanding.

We then laid down Gyeon Syncro, a 2 coat system, to protect the vehcile for 2+ yrs. To finish this detail off we gave the interior our signature interior valet, leaving it immaculate.

1 Step polish + coating

Dodge RAM

Josh, another loyal client, dropped his Dodge RAM off with us for a reset on his paint. A mixture road conditions, weather and poor washing methods leads to paint appearing duller than its full potential.

After talking with Josh, we decided to 1-step polish the RAM, as this would provide an all round improvement on the paint without chasing perfection on an older vehicle that had a past life in the USA.

For protection on this one, we used Gyeon Can Coat - a spray on ceramic product that provides a hydrophic durable layer up to 12mo.

New Car Light cut + 2yr Ceramic

'24 BMW X5

Stuart booked both of his cars with us back in 2022 for paint corrections and ceramic coatings. Early this year, he upgrated both cars, and sent them our way for the full treatment. This is his brand new X5

Brand new car, why polish it right? Factory paint, has a slight haze in it, due to the mass production of cars today, and the cure time needed in order to finish a car from start to finish asap.

A light cut polish is all that is needed here to give the pain that extra clarity, and remove any light marks from delivery of the vehicle to the delearship. We find often delearship pre-sale washes are all it takes to ruin the finish on your brand new car.

Not sure if your new car's paint is at its best? Contact us for a free inspection and quotation.

Wheel ceramic

BMW M3 E92

Our friend and loyal client over at MS Recovery & Repair, left us his BMW M3 to get the full Salt Valeting experience. This included the correction and ceramic of the carbon fibre roof, a 1 step polish on the paint followed by Gyeon Can Coat, and his new wheels ceramic coated front and back.

With paintwork showcased in above vehicles, lets focus on the wheels. Why Ceramic your wheels? Here are our top few benefits for you to conider.

Protection against contaminants - brake dust, road grime, tar deposits all increase the risk of corrosion

Easy Cleaning - Ceramic makes cleaning wheels so much more enjoyable and alot easier due to its extreme durability and hydrophic properties

Enhanced appearance - Ceramic adds that extra depth of gloss, giving your wheels that extra edge to avoid that dull/worn look.
