About Us

Subtitle here

Our main aim is to see souls won for Christ and help them grow and develop in their Christian life. To point people to gracious good news of the Gospel of Jesus Christ. Then to teach, encourage and strengthen their faith and to help Christians in their daily walk with the Lord. This is summed up in our statement,"Passion for Jesus, Compassion for people"
